Pizza

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F)

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F

  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.

  3. Pat and stretch dough onto a large pizza pan. Cover with desired toppings. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
